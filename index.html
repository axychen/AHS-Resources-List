Hello Soon-To-Be Grads!

Graduation is just around the corner! You’ve made it this far! So many people are proud of you, remember that!

We are so focused on “what’s next?” that sometimes we as students can find ourselves struggling with our mental, emotional, and physical health.

Below are multiple resources for you to check out ON CAMPUS that highlights positive mental health during your last semester here.

Empower Me is a mental health and wellness service that seeks to contribute to a resilient student community

More information: Empower Me

Mentor Assistance Through Education and Support provides support to students hoping to build social skills or experiencing personal, academic, or mental wellness difficulties.

More information: MATES

Off-Campus Community is a WUSA service for first and upper-year students living off-campus.

More information: Off-Campus Support

Be A Star

Be A Star draws on “scholarly research, logic, and the experience of Waterloo alumni” in order to help you take your talent in your domian to its highest expression.

More information: BE A STAR

Centre for Career Action

Centre for Career Action wants to help you learn about “career management techniques, graduate/professional school preparation, work search methods and how to set meaningful and achievable goals.” Visit the link below to see how they can help you prepare you for your future.

More information: Career Action
